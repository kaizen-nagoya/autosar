# ■ Additional Vehicle Safety Components & Functions

---

## ■ 1. Braking & Stability Systems

* **Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)**
  Prevents wheel lock during braking

* **Electronic Stability Control (ESC)**
  Prevents skidding and loss of control

* **Brake Assist**
  Boosts braking force in emergencies

* **Electronic Brakeforce Distribution**
  Optimizes brake force per wheel

---

## ■ 2. Structural Safety

* **Crumple Zone**
  Absorbs crash energy

* **Side Impact Beam**
  Protects occupants in side collisions

* **Safety Cage**
  Maintains cabin integrity

---

## ■ 3. Visibility & Awareness

* **Head-Up Display**
  Keeps driver’s eyes on the road

* **Adaptive Headlights**
  Illuminate curves and corners

* **Rearview Camera**
  Improves rear visibility

* **Defogging System**
  Maintains clear visibility

---

## ■ 4. Powertrain & Control Safety

* **Engine Control Unit**
  Controls engine safely

* **Fail-safe Mode**
  Allows safe operation during faults

* **Electronic Throttle Control**
  Prevents unintended acceleration

---

## ■ 5. Electrical & Communication Safety

* **CAN Bus**
  Reliable communication between ECUs

* **Redundant Power Supply**
  Ensures operation under failure

* **Battery Management System**
  Prevents overheating and failure in EVs

---

## ■ 6. Thermal & Fire Safety

* **Thermal Management System**
  Prevents overheating

* **Fuel Cut-off System**
  Stops fuel flow after collision

---

## ■ 7. Human Factors & Ergonomics

* **Steering Column Collapse Mechanism**
  Reduces injury during impact

* **Pedal Layout Design**
  Minimizes driver error

* **Seat Head Restraint**
  Prevents neck injury

---

## ■ 8. Post-Crash & Emergency Functions

* **Automatic Emergency Call System**
  Contacts emergency services after crash

* **Hazard Warning Lights**
  Alerts other drivers

* **Door Unlock System**
  Enables rescue access
